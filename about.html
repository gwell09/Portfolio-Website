About

Hi there! I'm Georgia.

I'm a senior at Parsons in New York City studying Game Design. My main areas of focus are technical and narrative design.

I have experience as a design intern working on AAA games for the Las Vegas Sphere, which develops games for more than 10,000 players. Before transitioning to the design team, I worked in quality assurance where I tested numerous prototypes for the Sphere across multiple platforms and also many popular games for Anthos Capital.

Aside from my professional work, I develop games in my own time, often leaning towards narrative or adventure games. I also enjoy animation and illustration, dabbling in pixel art and charcoal. Currently, I'm developing an adventure game about witch trials in contemporary times, and focusing on my level design, illustration, and writing.
