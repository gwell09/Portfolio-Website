Hi there! I'm Georgia.

I'm a student at Parsons in New York City studying Design and Technology, specifically game design. My main areas of focus are in general game design and environmental design. In the future, I'd love to lean into environmental design or possibly production.

I enjoy using lighting and camera perspectives to evoke a mood or convey a message. I also love a game with an interesting story that draws the completionist out of me! The Mass Effect Trilogy, a great example of this, is the series that inspired me to study the art of games and I've never looked back!
