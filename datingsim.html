NYC Dating Simulator
2022 | Unity
Imagine that dating in New York City is a minefield filled with loads of unsavory characters and some of the worst pickups imaginable. That wasn't so hard, was it?
Love in the city that never sleeps is a game of chance, chaos, and charisma. In this narrative-driven dating sim, you navigate a single day in New York, encountering a cast of insufferable stereotypical New York transplantsâ€”from the pretentious art school fashion student and Bushwick barista to the goth chick at the hyperpop concert. Each interaction could lead to romance, disaster, or just another wild NYC story.
  
  

Ideation

What makes this game unique from other dating sims is the fact that the characters are all supposed to be insufferable caricatures of young New York implants you might find out and about. Most of the characters are, in one way or another, unapproachable, irritable, and self-centered. However, they look cool and provide a challenge! This game has different ending screens based on who the player successfully romances. The replayability of the game was an important aspect.

I experimented with this game by using a pixel art style for the characters and at the time, I wasn't very experienced with 2D asset creation.

Emotions

This game is definitely more of a comedic experience as many of the dialogue options are rather silly or may not provide a reaction that the player expects based on the option they choose. Additionally, many of the references the characters make are not supposed to be understood by the player as many of them are niche artists or designers. This is supposed to recreate a very common feeling you can experience in New York of not feeling cool or trendy enough since many of the characters act like it's common knowledge.

Playtest Notes

I had about five or six friends play this game and the general feedback was very positive. The testers enjoyed the art and the characters. Many said they wished that some of the storylines were longer or more developed, but were still satisfied with the playthrough. Many also agreed that they enjoyed the replayability of the game, and found one or two of the characters provided a bit of a challenge to romance since they are prickly in nature.

Challenges

The greatest challenge for me here was simply creating the character art itself. I love to draw and have pretty developed skills, but I have not done much digital art, so I used this game as an opportunity to expand my abilities in digital art.
