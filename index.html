In a modern world gripped by an unexplained disease, fear, and superstition take hold. When our estranged sister—an outcast accused of witchcraft—begs for help, we must navigate a town consumed by hysteria, confront the church’s authority, and gain the trust of wary townsfolk. Blight is a Goreyesque 2D point-and-click Adventure game coming to Itch.io in Summer 2025.

Hi There!

I'm a senior at Parsons in New York City studying Game Design. My main areas of focus are technical and narrative design.

I have experience as a design intern working on AAA games for the Las Vegas Sphere. Before transitioning to the design team, I worked in quality assurance where I tested numerous prototypes for the Sphere, as well as many popular games for Anthos Capital. I have a love for rapid prototypes, illustration, animation, and adventure games!
